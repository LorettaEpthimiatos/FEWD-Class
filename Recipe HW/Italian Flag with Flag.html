ITALIAN GREENS AND BEANS

Description

Italian Greens and Beans has it all: it's very easy and inexpensive to make; it's highly nutritious; it's soulful and comforting; and it can be served as a main course, side dish, soup, or vegtable stew. My favorite way to serve this meal is as a main course with some Tuscan bread and oil.

Ingredients

The ingredients list below may be changed according to your preference. Some people prefer more garlic, sometimes less broth, but you can make any adjustment that works for you.

  • 1 Vidalia Onion
  • 3 medium garlic cloves (can be more or less to taste)
  • 4-6 Sausage Links
  • 2 48 ounce containers of chicken broth (again this can be more or less depending on how much liquid you prefer)
  • 4 cans of Cannellini Beans
  • 2 lbs of Spinach (Can substitute with Kale)

Instructions

  1. Dice the onion and saute until carmelized and soft.
  2. Mince and add garlic to onions.
  3. Peel the skin off the raw sausage and crumble as small as possible, then add to the onion and garlic.
  4. Cook all 3 ingredients till done and turn off heat.
  5. In a large pot add the 2 containers of chicken broth and bring to a boil.
  6. Add spinach to the chicken broth.
  7. After the spinach cooks down into the broth, add the meat mixture and the beans to the broth.
  8. Simmer for about 30-45 minutes.
  9. Place your Italian Greens and beans into bowls and top with parmesan cheese and enjoy.
